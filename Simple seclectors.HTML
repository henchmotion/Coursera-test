RULES AND REGULATIONS GUIDING A PASTOR AND A MINISTER IN KINGDOM DIPLOMATS

PASTORS AND MINISTERS' FUNCTIONS AND DUTIES

  1. Responsible for overseeing the effective functioning/ operations of the directorate under his supervision
  2. Responsible for overseeing the welfare and spiritual growth of the flock in the local assembly
  3. Co- Pastors the church and its effective administration and operations with the senior Pastors as assistant/ associate pastors in the local assembly
  4. Diligently leading prayer cells of his directorate Leading a pastoral care fellowship (Family Connect) committing to growing and effectively running a family connect that will be an example for the other leaders and workers to grow strong family connects
  5. Supervises the directors, HOD, and team leaders under his directorate to be faithful to their duties and receive their weekly reports to be presented in the operations/ pastors meeting.

RELATIONSHIP WITH CHURCH MEMBERS

  1. A Pastor/Minister must relate with the flock with the consciousness of being a spiritual leader and a priest over the flock representing the Senior Pastors and the Lord Jesus Christ
  2. A Pastor/Minister should not take advantage of the flock in whatever way, emotionally, physically, financially, etc.
  3. A Pastor/Minister is not permitted to borrow financially from the flock which includes members, workers, and leaders.
  4. In the case of doing business with any church member, the terms of the business must be well discussed and documented to protect the Pastor/Minister from being accused of taking advantage of any of the brethren. Also, let the Pastor/Minister commit to keeping the terms in the agreement.
